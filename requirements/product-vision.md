# Product Vision
Fast Video Captioning helps content creators generate accurate captions quickly while maintaining human oversight. AI accelerates the captioning process, but creators remain in control of final published content.

The system learns from user corrections over time, allowing channel-specific terminology, names, and phrases to improve caption quality while preserving transparency and accountability.

The goal is not fully autonomous caption generation. The goal is reducing manual effort while supporting responsible AI practices through review, feedback, and continuous improvement.
